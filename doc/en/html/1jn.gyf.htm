Kinetic Models - Two-Component, Second-Order Decay

R = Kb C (C - CL)

This model assumes that substance A reacts with substance B in some unknown ratio to produce a product P. The rate of disappearance of A is proportional to the product of A and B remaining. CL can be either positive or negative, depending on whether either component A or B is in excess, respectively. This model sometimes shows improved fits to chlorine decay data that does not conform to the simple first-order decay model.
