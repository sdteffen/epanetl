Analysis Options - Energy

Energy Options provide default values used to compute pumping energy and cost when no specific energy parameters are assigned to a given pump. They include:

Pump Efficiency

Default pump efficiency (as a percent).

Energy Price

Price of energy per kilowatt-hour. Monetary units are not explicitly represented.

Price Pattern

ID label of a time pattern used to represent variations in energy price with time.

Demand Charge

Additional energy charge per maximum kilowatt usage.
